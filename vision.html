Grace Vision

Understand His Grace. Grow in His Grace. Work in His Grace.

Grace Journal is a free spiritual growth app. It is not a replacement for your pastor, your Bible or your prayer life. It is a tool to help you deepen your faith. That is it. Everything inside Grace Journal is built around one word. GRACE.

GGrowth

start here ✦

Spiritual growth is one of the most important things to us. We believe that if you call yourself a Christian, beyond anything else, this should be one of your top priorities.

Right now, church can feel like a consumeristic space. People come in, listen to a sermon, and walk out not really remembering what happened — almost like a movie at the cinema. You showed up, you sat there, you left. We believe the way this changes is by encouraging note taking — actually writing down what stood out, and what you are going to work on. Because that is where growth starts.

That is where the SPARK moment comes in. Everyone has a moment where something hits them — a sermon that convicts them, a friend that reminds them to get back into the Word, a worship song that breaks something open. Grace Journal is not trying to create the spark — God does that. We are just here for the moment after, to make sure it does not go to waste.

RReflection

your notes ✦

Reflection is our note-taking feature, and one of the core reasons Grace Journal exists. We want to encourage people to write down the things that stick out to them — what they felt convicted about, and what they are actually going to do differently.

Because a note you come back to on Wednesday is worth more than ten sermons you forgot by Sunday night. It is not just about taking notes either. It is about reviewing them, applying them, and letting what God spoke actually change something in your life.

AAttitude

gratitude ✦

This is our gratitude feature, and this one really matters to us. We are a generation of more — raised in a world constantly trying to sell us something. More of this. The newest that. It has led to a rise in self-comparison, and with it, anxiety.

Gratitude is a way to slow down. Take a moment. Take a breath. Away from the noise, reflect and write down what God has blessed you with, and be truly grateful.

Grace Map. Connected to gratitude, Grace Map is a living picture of everything God has blessed you with. Every time you write down something you are grateful for, it becomes a node on your map — a point of light. The more you mention something, the larger it grows, showing you where God is moving most in your life. When two blessings appear on the same day, a line forms between them — threads of grace woven through your days. In a world pushing us toward more, Grace Map exists to show you what you already have.

CConfidence

boldness ✦

We believe the Word of God is powerful, alive and active. We believe there is power in prayer, power in declaring scripture, and power in speaking life over yourself. The prayer and declarations feature is where this comes to life. It is not about having the perfect words — it is about showing up, being honest with God, and praying with the Word behind you.

This is also where GraceAI comes in, and we want to be clear about what it is and what it is not. GraceAI does not write your prayers, your declarations, or your gratitude. It is a signpost, not a shepherd. What it does is remove friction — surfacing scriptures you can actually stand on and declare over a situation, so your boldness is built on the Word and not just a feeling.

EEngagement

show up ✦

Engagement is about building a real daily relationship with scripture. Not just reading it — actually engaging with it, sitting with it, letting it speak to you. Every feature in Grace Journal points back to this.

Because the hardest thing in a Christian's journey is not understanding the Word. It is consistency. Consistent Bible reading. Consistent prayer. Consistent gratitude. Consistent follow-through on what God is speaking. That is what Grace Journal is here for.

Who is Grace Journal for?

Everyone. Whether you are 19 and just finding your faith, 45 with kids wanting to reignite that fire, a prodigal who has just come back, or someone finding their way back. Whatever your walk of faith, whatever season you are in — Grace Journal is for you.

And it is completely free. We are bridging the gap. All you need to do is download it. Grace Journal is funded through donations, grants and church partnerships. We are not under any pressure to monetise this. We are completely focused on the impact.

What does success look like?

not numbers ✦

Not downloads. Not numbers. If the person using the app becomes more consistent — engaging with the Word, going back to their sermon notes and actually applying them — we believe that leads to a deeper hunger for the Word. Not just better habits. A deeper hunger.

Success is a teenager who used to scroll through their phone during worship now writing in Grace Journal because they want to remember what God is saying to them. Success is someone who had a spark six months ago and, instead of letting it fade, actually built a habit around it.
