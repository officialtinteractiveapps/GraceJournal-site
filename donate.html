Free because impact comes first

Funded by people who believe in the mission

Grace Journal is, and will stay, completely free. We are not under any pressure to monetise this — we are completely focused on the impact. The app is funded through donations, grants and church partnerships. If it has been a blessing to you, you can help keep it a blessing for the next person.

1Where your giving goes

straight to impact ✦

Every gift goes directly into keeping Grace Journal free and getting it into more hands — servers and infrastructure, the AI features that point people back to the Word, and building out the tools that help people stay consistent in their faith.

We are not building this to get rich. We are bridging a gap. 100% of giving serves that mission.

2Ways to give

every bit helps ✦

One-off or monthly. A single gift helps today; a monthly gift helps us plan and keep the lights on for the long haul.

Church partnerships. If your church wants to come alongside the mission, we would love to talk about partnering together.

Grants & sponsorship. If you represent a foundation or organisation that supports faith and wellbeing initiatives, reach out.

3A note on stewardship

honest with it ✦

We take giving seriously and want to honour it. We are committed to being transparent about how funds are used, and to keeping the app free as a result. You are trusting us, and we are trusting God to provide through it.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.” — 2 Corinthians 9:7
